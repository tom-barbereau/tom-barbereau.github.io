I research the sociology and governance of information technology, with a particular interest in decentralised (quasi distributed) systems. I provide answers to organisational and policy questions in their application and implications. I have a background in information systems management (PhD) and sociology of technologies (MSc).

I work as a scientist at the Dutch Organisation for Applied Scientific Research, Centre for Societal Innovation and Strategy. I was a researcher at the Interdisciplinary Centre for Reliability, Security, and Trust (University of Luxembourg) and visiting researcher the Institute for Information Law (University of Amsterdam).
