Welcome to my personal website!

My name is Tom Barbereau, I am a social scientist with a keen interest in emerging technologies and dynamics of innovation. I hold an M.Sc. in Science & Technology Studies (University of Edinburgh) and Ph.D. in Information Systems Management (University of Luxembourg).

Having successfully collaborated and published with lawyers, mathematicians, and computer scientists, my strength lies in cross-disciplinary collaboration, to find logical relations between topics, and to take apart socio-technological controversies.

My research focuses on the governance of technology, and in particular decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs). His critical research extends to the metaverse and self-sovereign identity systems. Over the years, I contributed impactful research featured in Technology in Society, Philosophy and Technology, and Government Information Quarterly.

I also published at leading conferences and contributed to edited books.
