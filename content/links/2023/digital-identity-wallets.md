A great article published by my former colleague Dr. [Reilly Smethurst](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=BT7TNZEAAAAJ&hl=en) on the semantic confusions and contradictions surrounding self-sovereign identity. 

> In 2021 and 2022, wallet-based digital identity projects received support from Big Tech companies and large European governments. Notable examples include the Web3 wallets and fictional identities
supported by Meta and Twitter, the Block-Microsoft Web5 SSI Service, the European Diploma pilot,
and Germany’s failed ID Wallet-App. These projects exhibit significant differences, yet they are all
associated with “SSI” or “self-sovereignty”. [...]
>
